Video Format Mix

Long-Form + Shorts Strategy

Long-form (8–20 min) builds authority and drives search traffic. Shorts (under 60 sec) drive subscriber growth and reach new audiences. Run both simultaneously from Day 1.

  • Long-form: 1× per week — tutorials, build-alongs, strategy breakdowns
  • Shorts: 2× per week — quick tips, AI hacks, product demos, behind-the-scenes
  • Community posts: 2–3× per week — polls, announcements, resource drops
